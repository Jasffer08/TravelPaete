Trivia

Famous for woodcrafts and lanzones fruit, the town of Paete is also rich in history,
from the time when it was a village ruled by Gat Lacampauid to the present.
The name is said to come from ‘paet’ which means chisel. The priest asked the name
of the place but the villager thought he was asking the name of his tool. Although the
christening is accidental, the name is very appropriate considering that Paete is the
‘woodcarving capital of the Philippines’. Some accounts even say that the world-
famous yo-yo had its origins in Paete!

Why You Need To Visit Paete?
Itis known for its generations of skilled artisans and
their woodcarvings—from life-size statues of saints,
to miniature sculptures and wall hangings.
